What is Computing?

Computing, in the context of computer science, refers to the process of using computer systems to solve problems. This involves the design and use of algorithms (step-by-step procedures), data structures (ways to organize and store data), and computer systems. It can include a wide range of tasks, from simple calculations to complex tasks like artificial intelligence and machine learning.
